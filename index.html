Intro to DG

Group of three students from IIIT-BH started a club named it as DG community.

The main aim of this group is to indulge themselves in new & emerging technologies as a hobby may eventually developing cutting edge technologies.

This website will be the basic medium for you to collabarate with us and contacting us .If you have a potential ideas and having passion for developing with us then you are very welcome .

If you are intrested, please let us know by registering with us in DG Community or Email us

Join the 'IRC'(Internet Relay Chat) channel in DG Community section, join the discussion for more insights. The RIOT IRC helps you to collabarate with us even if you don't have idea about version control you can simply upload code as files
